# Feature Ideas
- [ ] Ability to take photos and keep track of your progress
- [ ] Ability to swap exercise mid workout
- [ ] Customizing the AI trainer to "call out out" or send notifications or other interactions - user should be able to customize how "aggressive" or "supportive" or "encouraging" the AI is. E.g. - if the user clicks "skip" while working out, the AI could be more supportive or more aggressive depending on their preference - opens as a notification bubble. Could also be an opportunity to get feedback from users on how the workout is going and if we should continue to or not recommend the same / similar exercises.
